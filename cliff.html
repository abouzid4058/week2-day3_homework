California's Greatest Cliffs

The greatest cliffs are these four cliffs located in Southern California. Each of these cliffs has its own uniqueness and beauty: West Cliff, North Cliff, East Cliff, and South Cliff are the pride of California. Each cliff offers many amenities to visitors such as recreational areas, camping sites, wildlife watch spots, and overnight cabins.

No matter which direction you head from Irvine, California, the sites are easily reached within a 100-mile radius. West Cliff is the most popular among Westcliff University campus students because of its close proximity.
